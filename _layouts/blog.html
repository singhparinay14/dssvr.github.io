Research Questions


Some of the research questions that we will focus on in this project are mentioned here, which we will try to find the answer to as close as possible.


Q. What are the potential benefits and drawbacks of digitizing sacred spaces through virtual reality?


Q. How does the VR environment influence interaction between people?

- Do people interact more?

- Can the virtual experience increase understanding/acceptance for other religions and their practices/beliefs?

- Do the users feel that they communicate on a better level in VR than in reality/other mediums?


Q. How does virtual reality technology change the way that people engage with and understand sacred spaces/objects and their history?

- How does the use of virtual reality in sacred spaces compare to other forms of digital media, such as video or photographs, in terms of their effectiveness in transmitting the essence and meaning of the spaces?

- Do people use the opportunity to engage with objects they cannot engage with in reality?

- Do people make use of "supernatural" usage of objects?

- Can the virtual experience increase understanding/acceptance for other religions and their practices/beliefs?


Q. How can virtual reality be used to enhance the experience of visiting sacred spaces for those who are unable to physically visit them due to distance or accessibility issues?

- Does the virtual experience decrease the feeling of loneliness/isolation?

- Do the users feel included?

- Do the users feel more connected to their faith through these experiences?


Q. How does a VR learning experience differ from the conventional methods when it comes to religious preaching for children?

- Do the users show more interest in things than in reality?

- Does the virtual experience increase the amount of time that users interact/engage?


Q. What are the ethical considerations of using virtual reality in sacred spaces, and how can these be addressed?

- Does the virtual experience provide the same feelings/emotions as the real one does?

- How do users feel about using VR as a substitute for the real experience?


Q. How can sacred spaces be digitized using VR?

- What technologies can/should (not) be used?

- What are/were the challenges?
