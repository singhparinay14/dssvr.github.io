Timeline



October 2022

Project Kickoff

Project was kicked off by Mr. Enes Yigitbas with introduction to the project group member and getting to know about the technologies that we were going to work on, along with the different phases of the project period.

October 2022 - November 2022

Tutorial Phase

This phase focused on getting all the group members warmed up and defining the requirements and goals of DSS-VR.

November 2022 - December 2022

Research Phase

Each group member conducted research individually on different topics such as Unity, Blender, Meta Quest/Oculus Development Kit, Potential DSS-VR Use Cases, VR tourism and Sacred Spaces and Their Representation, which were later presented to the whole group to get everyone at the same knowledge level.

December 2022

Beginning of Implementation

All the information collected during the research phase was combined with the refined requirements and goals, and implementation was initiated.

Present

Development Phase

The application has started taking shape and the environment has been set up, the first version should be ready soon for testing.

Coming Months

Testing & User Feedback

Application will be tested in different environments and with different users.
